Basic Questions
Q1: What is the purpose of the proposed smart video surveillance system?

The system is designed to enforce safety measures in closed gated communities such as smart cities, malls, and universities by tracking, monitoring, identifying, and detecting vehicles and license plates in real-time.

Q2: What are the primary components of the smart video surveillance system?

The system consists of four primary components: vehicle monitoring and data storage, an accelerated search process, real-time alerts for accidents and violations, and periodic data analysis.

Q3: How does the system work?

The proposed solution utilizes deep learning and computer vision techniques through a network of IP cameras to track and monitor vehicles and license plates. It employs an architecture with interconnected subsystems including camera, processing, database, alerting, searching, and analytics dashboard.
